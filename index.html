How does the 7-day free trial work?
+
Click "Start 7-day free trial" → we verify your card with a tiny ₹1 charge (refunded instantly) → you get FULL Pro access for 7 days. On Day 6, we send a clear reminder that you'll be billed tomorrow. On Day 7, you're charged ₹299 only if you don't cancel. No sneaky charges, ever. Cancel anytime by typing /cancel in the bot.
How is this different from ChatGPT?
+
Three big differences: (1) AskGogo uses Claude AI — smarter reasoning and better instruction-following than ChatGPT. (2) It has persistent memory that grows with you — ChatGPT forgets after every conversation. (3) It takes action — fires reminders, syncs calendar, manages lists. ChatGPT just talks.
Is my data private?
+
Yes, absolutely. Your memories and conversations are stored encrypted in your own dedicated database. We never sell data, never show ads, never share with third parties. You can delete everything with one command (/wipe). GDPR-compliant, security-first architecture.
Do I need to install an app?
+
No. AskGogo lives inside Telegram (and WhatsApp soon), which you already have. Click the bot link, press Start, and talk naturally. Nothing to install, nothing to learn. It just works.
Can I connect my Google Calendar?
+
Yes. On any paid plan, type /calendar and connect your Google account in 10 seconds. Events you create via AskGogo appear in Calendar instantly. Events from Calendar show up in your bot's daily briefing. True two-way sync.
When will WhatsApp be supported?
+
WhatsApp launches next month. Meta requires proof of active users before approving Business API — we're in final review. Once live, every existing user gets free WhatsApp access. Follow @askgogo_bot on Telegram for the announcement.
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Yes. Type /cancel in the bot or cancel from your web dashboard. You'll keep Pro access until your billing cycle ends, then automatically downgrade to Free plan. All your memories stay intact forever.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. Data Controller

AskGogo is operated by Goverdhan M D, Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. Contact: goverdhan@tipplr.in | +91 83104 41698

2. What We Collect

We collect only data you explicitly share with the bot: messages, memories, reminders, lists, voice notes, and photos. We also store your Telegram/WhatsApp user ID and display name. We do NOT collect browsing data, location, contacts, or any data beyond what you directly send to AskGogo.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide AskGogo services: storing memories, sending reminders, managing lists, syncing calendar events, and generating AI responses. We never sell your data, display advertisements, or share your information with third parties for marketing.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) with encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest. Voice notes are processed via OpenAI Whisper API and deleted after transcription. AI conversations are processed via Anthropic Claude API with enterprise-grade security.

5. Third-Party Services

AskGogo uses: Telegram Bot API (messaging), Twilio (WhatsApp messaging), Anthropic Claude API (AI), OpenAI Whisper API (voice transcription), Google Calendar API (calendar sync), Supabase (database), Vercel (hosting), Razorpay (payments). Each operates under its own privacy policy.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Data is retained while your account is active. Delete all data anytime by sending /wipe to the bot. Upon deletion, all memories, conversations, reminders, and lists are permanently removed within 24 hours.

7. WhatsApp and Telegram Data

Messages sent via WhatsApp are processed through Twilio. Messages via Telegram use the Telegram Bot API. We store message content to provide memory and conversation features. We do not access your contacts, call history, or any data beyond messages you send directly to AskGogo.

8. Google Calendar

If you connect Google Calendar, we store only a refresh token. We read and create events as requested. We never modify or delete events without your instruction. Disconnect anytime.

9. Payments

Payments are processed by Razorpay (PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant). AskGogo does not store credit card numbers, bank details, or UPI IDs.

10. Cookies

We use only one essential cookie for theme preference (light/dark mode). No tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies.

11. Children

AskGogo is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

12. Your Rights

You can: access your data (/memory), rectify data, delete data (/wipe), restrict processing, request data portability, and object to processing. Contact goverdhan@tipplr.in to exercise these rights.

13. Changes

We may update this policy. Significant changes are communicated via the bot.

14. Contact
